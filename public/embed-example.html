How it works:

  1. Add <script src="/widget.js"></script> before closing </body> tag
  2. The widget loads automatically and appears in the bottom right corner
  3. Users click the SupportAI icon to open the chat
  4. They can ask questions and get AI-powered responses
  5. You can customize colors and position from your SupportAI dashboard
